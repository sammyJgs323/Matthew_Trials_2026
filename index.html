    08

    Operating rules

    Seven non-negotiables. They cost nothing and protect the marks the plan is designed to earn.

    Exam-room protocol
    1. Read the whole paper first — 3 minutes. Know the terrain before you start.
    2. Allocate time by marks — roughly 1 mark ≈ 1–1.5 min. Watch the clock at every section.
    3. Bank the easy marks first. Do what you know cold, then return to the hard questions.
    4. Show every step — method marks in Maths & Physics are free marks. Never just write the answer.
    5. Never leave a blank. A written attempt can score; an empty line cannot.
